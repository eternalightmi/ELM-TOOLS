⚠ SMART HUB-300 — total capacity is 300 VA across all three zones. Maximum load per individual output line is 130 VA. Do not put more than 130 VA on any single zone.

⚠ Cable: SMART HUB-300 requires 10/2 cable only. Maximum cable run is 525 ft (160m) per line — double that of other hubs.

⚠ Fixture caution: Do not connect DB-LED, HYVE 22, or FUSION 22 fixtures to the SMART HUB-300. Use DOT and DOT 22 for those fixture types instead.

⚠ Cable: SMART HUB-300 requires 10/2 cable only — no 14/2 or 12/2. Maximum run is 525 ft (160m) per line.

Max VA is hub-TOTAL across all outputs combined — not per port. Each port gets its own full cable-length budget independently.

⚠ Special Fixture Rules🔦
·BIG NERO / BIG NERO NARROW — must use 10/2 cable, max 40m (131 ft) cable run, and count limits apply: HUB-75 max 4, HUB-100 max 7, SMART HUB-150 max 11 total (max 7 per line). These override normal VA calculations.
·SMART fixtures (SMART SCOPE TONE, SMART FLUX TONE, SMART EVO FLEX TONE, etc.) only work on SMART HUBs — will not function on HUB-75 or HUB-100.
·SMART HUB-300 — requires 10/2 cable only. Max run 525 ft (160m) per line. Do not connect DB-LED, HYVE 22, or FUSION 22.
·Punctured cable? Cut it completely. Rejoin with CC-2. After one CC-2 max downstream load is 50 VA. After two CC-2 connectors max is 100 VA.
·Branching — always use the same cable type on both sides of a branch. Never mix 14/2 and 10/2 at a junction.
·Non-in-lite components void the warranty entirely. Only use in-lite fixtures, cables, and connectors within the system.
·Easy-Lock gel — the connector contains a moisture-protection gel. Wash hands after handling. Avoid eye contact.
Key Rules📋
·Branch distance = segment length + all parent segments back to hub. A 50 ft trunk + 40 ft branch = 90 ft cumulative.
·35 VA on a branch → step up to 10/2 regardless of distance.
·50+ VA through a CC-2 → use two CC-2 connectors at that junction, not one.
·Balance zones — spread VA evenly across outputs. Hub total is the hard limit.
·Test above ground before burying any cable.
